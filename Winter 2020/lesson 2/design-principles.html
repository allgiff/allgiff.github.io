Design Principles

Contrast

Juliana Biciycles

This website utilizes contrast through dark colors and pictures against a white background. The dark colors contrast with the main white of the site. It is clear and easy to read.

Visual Hierarchy

Deskpass

This website shows visual hierarchy through the format. Your eyes go to the main headline and brings your eye down through the rest of the content. It is stacked, which shows clear hierarchy.

Proximity

Los Angeles Times

It is important for sites that give the news to have clear proximity. It helps the viwers understand which headlines and images are realated through their proximity to each other. The Los Angeles Times is a good example of this.
