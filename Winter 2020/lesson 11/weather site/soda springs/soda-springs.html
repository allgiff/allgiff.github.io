The Geyser

The City of Soda Springs has the geyser set on a timer to go off every hour on the hour. You can see the Geyser today blow 70 feet into the air and hear it roar. The Geyser was discovered in 1937, when a crew of workers was breaking ground on a new project. Imagine their surprise when a jet of hot water burst out of the ground!

The Soda Springs Geyser even had an effect on the world famous "Old Faithful." It was throwing it off schedule!

Soda Springs is also fameous for several mineral springs surrounding the area, such as Octagon Springs and Lovers Delight. They make for an excelent spa day!

The Geyser is open year round and costs no fee. For more information contact city hall.
