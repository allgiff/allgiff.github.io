Tortilla Soup

    Ingredients:
  • 3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 3/4 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 chicken bouillon cube
  • 2 T butter, melted
  • 14 oz stewed, diced tomatoes
  • 1 can chicken broth
  • 3 potatoes, diced
  • 3 carrots
  • ½ c. corn
  • Salt & pepper
  • 2 tsp taco seasoning

Instructions:
Sauté onions and garlic powder in butter until onions are tender. Meanwhile, in a large pot, cook chicken in just enough boiling water to cover. Cool and chop chicken. Save broth and add bouillon cubes to warm broth. Add chopped chicken and remaining soup ingredients. Bring to a boil and simmer until carrots and potatoes are tender. Pour into bowls and garnish with cheese, sour cream, and tortilla chips

Bacon Broccoli Alfredo Pasta

    Ingredients:
  • 3 T. butter
  • 2 oz. cream cheese
  • 1/2 c. Milk
  • 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 T. Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 3 slices of bacon
  • Pasta

Instructions:
Steam broccoli and cook bacon set both aside. Boil pasta. Heat butter, cream cheese, milk, italian seasoning, salt, sugar, garlic powder, and parmasean cheese in a sauce pan on medium-low heat (in order.) When sauce has thickened add broccoli, bacon and pasta. Stir until well combined. Dish onto plates and top with crumbled bacon if desired.
