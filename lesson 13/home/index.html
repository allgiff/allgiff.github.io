While you prepare for your temple trip, we are preparing for you. We have many services and accomodations to meet your needs, and will be here for you. We are ready to help, and we are looking forward to your stay with us.

Temple Inn & Suites caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized service and accomodations that meet the needs of those patrons. These hotel accomodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by our company. If you have any questions please give us a call, (123) 456-7890.

The Portland temple is located in the beautiful state of Oregon. It was the first temple in Oregon, announced in 1984, and dedicated in 1989 by President Gordon B. Hinkley.

Due to Corona Virus the temple was closed in 2020, and will remain closed until large gatherings are deemed safe.
