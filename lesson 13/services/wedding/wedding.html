Reception Accomadations

Looking for a place to hold the reception? Look no further! Temple Inns and Suites has the finest accomidations for wedding receeptions in the area.

We want your special day to be stress free. Our professional staff will help you with every detail, so you can enjoy your day without any worries!
Reception hall prices start at $1000. Enjoy our reception hall, which can be deccorated according to the theme and needs of the wedding party.

We also offer a food catering service, so don't be afraid to bring in some hungry guests. Wedding catering services start at $500 and increase depending on the amount of people and selected food. An additional fee of $25 dollar will be added for every additional extra guest. Please consider us for your reception!

If you have any questions about the catering service, feel free to contact us via our contact page.
