Audience

The target audience is adults who live in southern Idaho, and are wondering how safe the roads are, and how far a storm is.

Personas

Jack Evans:

Jack is a meteorologist for a local news channel is southern Idaho. He loves looking at the weather updates on websites because they help him stay informed. He mainly wants to know about storms because he has a storm chaser hobby.

Lynn Miles:

Lynn is a school teacher at Oakwood Elementary in Preston Idaho. She loves teaching children and wants to plan ahead so she can safely make it to school. She needs to know when the roads are icy and snowy so she can adjust when she leaves for work.

Scenarios

This website will address the following senarios:

  1. How much ice is on the roads?
  2. Will I be able to leave for work later is it's snowy?
  3. When is the next storm?
  4. How bad will the next storm be?
  5. How far away am I from the storm?
