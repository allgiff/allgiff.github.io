Bear Lake

Head out to Fish Haven and get into nature! Fish Haven has many beautiful hikes, and sits on the shores of Bear Lake. Rent a lodge on the lake shore and enjoy the vacation!

Fish Haven has many options for an adventurous vacation. There are beautiful hikes, lovely hotels, and a lake to relax next to. Bear lake is a main attraction for the town, and brings in many visitors looking for a relaxing stay.

But, travelers beware. Within the lake lurks a beast, a sea monster. There is not a lot of information about the creature. It is rather elusive and sightings are rare. It was described as a serpent-like creature, similar to the monster of Flathead lake. Many think it to be peaceful, while others are convinced that it is dangerous. Travelers beware, if you come across the creature, you will never wish to return.

Bear lake is a beautiful place, with many options for hotels. Have fun relaxing on the shore, and enjoy your stay! For more tourist information, such as lodge options, visit the Bear Lake website.
