The Festival Was a Hit

Preston, Idaho is one of the best kept secrets of the West. Nestled on the Idaho side of beautiful Cache Valley and surrounded by towering mountains. The town was quiet, and a great place to relax. Then Napoleon Dynamite came along

Suddenly the town was famous, filled with fans coming to see the city where a great movie was filmed. Because of this, the Napoleon Dynamite festival was born.

Many of the featured festival themes related to events occurring during the film. For example: Tetherball Tournament, Tater Tot Eating Contest, Moon Boot Dance, Impersonation, Look-A-Like Contest, Football Throwing Contest and more.

In addition to brining in tourists, and raising the town's popularity, the festival was able to raise over $1,000 for the local schools. It was a great event that fans were happy to be a part of.
